Healthcare cybersecurity concerns worsened by pandemic

Health information related security breaches increased in the past decade, with 2020 having the highest number of individual incidents in a year so far. Some attribute the rise to cybersecurity weaknesses worsened by the pandemic. Healthcare is the number one sector for breaches, revealing the high but unattainable demand on the cybersecurity workforce.
